Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Data Collection

Mirror for Photoshop does not collect or store any personal information. Your preview images and creative work never leave your local network.

Network Communication

The app connects to a relay server running on your local network to receive preview images from Photoshop. All preview data stays within your local network.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

The iOS app uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to help us improve stability and fix bugs. This includes:

Crash reports: Stack traces and device information when the app crashes, helping us identify and fix issues.

Diagnostic data: Connection errors and app performance metrics to improve reliability.

Usage events: Anonymous events like successful or failed connections (not your preview content).

Device identifiers: Anonymous IDs for analytics, not linked to your personal identity.

This data is processed by Google Firebase and is used solely to improve the app. It is not used for advertising or tracking. You can limit analytics collection in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.
