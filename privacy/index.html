Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Data Collection

Mirror for Photoshop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your local network.

Network Communication

Mirror for Photoshop connects to a relay server running on your local network to receive preview images from Photoshop. All communication stays within your local network. No data is sent to external servers.

Analytics

Mirror for Photoshop does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
